DataScope – Access Control and Time & Attendance Systems

DataScope is one of the UK’s leading providers of temporary site access control and time & attendance systems for the construction sector, supporting some of the largest and most prestigious construction companies all over the UK.

What we offer

complete service

We provide a total service from installation, data hosting, hourly backups, onsite maintenance, training, helpdesk support, customisation as a project evolves, all the way through to off hire and collection.

Unique Solutions

Our hardware and software is manufactured in house and built specifically for purpose. This enables us to tailor each system to suit each client.

limitless reporting

The software interface can capture induction data from personal information, health and medical details, competency and training to mode of transport, miles travelled to site and CO2 emissions. Combined with a detailed account of operative’s movements and hours worked, the number of possible reports is limitless.

waeless capability

Our Access Control equipment only requires power, no internet connection or unnecessary wiring between devices is required. We also offer remotely accessible IP CCTV systems.

intuitive interface

Our software interface does not require installation or onsite maintenance. It is web based and can be accessed securely 24 hours a day for reporting, emergency muster reporting or for remote data entry, with contractor login capability. Additional modules include Delivery Management, Asset Inspection Register (AIR), Electronic Work Request and Scaffold Management.
